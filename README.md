HDFC Bank – Brand Positioning & Marketing Communication Analysis

This project evaluates the brand positioning, digital presence, and marketing communication strategy of HDFC Bank within the Indian banking sector.

Key Areas Covered

Competitive Brand Comparison

Marketing Channel Intensity Analysis

Digital & Social Media Presence

SWOT Analysis (Brand Perspective)

Strategic Recommendations

Tools Used

Google Sheets (Secondary Data Analysis)

Pages (Report Design)

Public Brand Communication Observation
Objective

To analyze HDFC Bank’s brand communication effectiveness and propose strategic improvements aligned with evolving digital and youth-oriented market trends.
